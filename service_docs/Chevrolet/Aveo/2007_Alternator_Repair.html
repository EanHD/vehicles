🔍 Common Issues & Troubleshooting

Alternator Failure: Often due to worn-out brushes or bearings. Symptoms include dimming headlights or dead battery. Solution: Replace the alternator.
Belt Slippage: Caused by improper belt tension or worn-out belt. Symptoms include squealing noise or overheating engine. Solution: Adjust or replace the serpentine belt.
