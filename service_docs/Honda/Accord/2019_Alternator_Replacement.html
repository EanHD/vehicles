üîç Common Issues & Troubleshooting

Intermittent charging: Loose or corroded battery/alternator connections. Clean terminals and ensure tight connections.
Belt squeal after replacement: Incorrect belt tension or routing. Double-check belt diagram and tensioner operation.
Alternator not charging: Faulty replacement unit or incorrect installation. Verify all connections and test alternator output with engine running.
Check charging system light: Often caused by a failing alternator, but also check battery health and all wiring harnesses for damage.
Difficulty removing alternator: Tight clearance around A/C lines and power steering reservoir. Temporarily move components as needed for access.
